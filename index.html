Benefit score = weighted avg of Impact, Criticality, Value ($)
Friction score = weighted avg of Difficulty, Time to value, Risk, Dependencies
Priority score = Benefit × (6 − Friction) — rewards high benefit + low friction
Priority index = Benefit ÷ Friction — pure bang-for-buck ratio
Priority rank = ranked descending by Priority score
